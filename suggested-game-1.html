You Should Play Minecraft

You are now interested in playing video games during Covid19. But you do not know which games are good. One of the top games I recommend for new players and veteran players alike is Minecraft (Minecraft is one of the few games not on Steam). For those of you that have not played much video games you may have already heard of this game. For those of you that have played a lot of video games, this game needs no introduction. Minecraft is a game where everything is made out of blocks. You might be wondering if you can play this game. Do not fret, Minecraft has game ports for everything from IOS, Android, Windows, MAC OS, Playstation 4 and Xbox One. For those of you who do not know much about computers and do not know much about pc requirements, do not worry. Minecraft is not that resource intensive of a game. However, when I see the specs requirement on that website, I think you can get away with a slightly weaker powered laptop. There are a few ways to play Minecraft. It is both a single player game and multiplayer game. There is a survival mode which you gather resources and try to make the world how you want, while fighting bosses. There is also a creative mode which is basically how it sounds. You do you get to fly around and have access to unlimited resources in your inventory. The goal of creative mode is to create whatever you want. If you need help in the game, there are countless Minecraft YouTube videos.
